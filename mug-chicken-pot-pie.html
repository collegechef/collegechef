Mug Chicken Pot Pie

Cost: $15.00

Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon Earth Balance Original Buttery Spread
  • 1/2 cup chopped mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 tablespoon flour
  • 2 tablespoons skim milk
  • 2 tablespoons chicken stock
  • 1/2 cooked chicken breast, cut into small pieces
  • 3 spears cooked asparagus, cut into small pieces
  • 4 sheets filo
  • Low-calorie cooking spray
  • 1 tsp. chopped fresh parsley

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F.

    2. Microwave the margarine and mixed vegetables in a microve- or oven-safe mug for 1 minute, or until the butter has melted.

    3. Add in the flour and stir until a paste has formed. Then stir in the skim milk and chicken stock.

    4. Add the chiciken and asparagus to the mug and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper.

    5. Cut out 4 squares of filo from one sheet, making sure each square is slightly larger than the top of the mug.

    6. Place the mug in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes until the filo is flaky and golden in color.

    7. Sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
