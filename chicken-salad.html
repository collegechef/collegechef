Chicken Salad

Time: 10 minutes

Cost: $12

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2-3 cups or 3/4 lb chopped, cooked chicken
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 4-6 green olives, pitted and
    mincedmince: to cut or chop into very small pieces. Source
  • 1/4 cup of chopped red onion
  • 1/2 to a whole apple, cored and chopped
  • 1/3 head of iceberg head lettuce, sliced and chopped

    • Dressing:

  • 5 Tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp plum preserves, or any sweet berry preserve (or a lesser amount of honey)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions

    To Cook the Chicken:

    1. Start with a pound of boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into large (2-3 inch) pieces.

    2. Bring a 2 quart pot of well-salted water to a

    rolling boilrolling boil: when a liquid, usually water, is boiling and so many bubbles are being produced so fast from the bottom of the pot that stirring won't impact their production. Source
    . Add the chicken to the boiling water, turn off the heat and cover the pot.

    3. Let the chicken pieces sit in the hot water for 15 minutes, then drain the water and chop the chicken pieces into smaller pieces for the salad.

    To Make the Salad:

    1. Prep all of the salad ingredients and toss them together in a large bowl.

    2. Prepare the chicken salad dressing in a separate smaller bowl. Mix together the mayonnaise, preserves, and lemon juice.

    3. Taste for the proper balance of sweetness and acidity. The salad dressing should not be too sweet nor too sour. Add more preserves or lemon juice until you have reached the balance you want. Add salt and pepper to taste.

    4. Gently stir the dressing in with the chicken salad ingredients. Taste and add more salt and pepper if needed.
