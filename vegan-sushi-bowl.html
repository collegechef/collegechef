Vegan Sushi Bowl

Time: 15 minutes

Cost: $13

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sushi Rice (or other short-grain rice)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 2 teaspoon natural cane sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup each: Carrots, cucumber, avocado, and pepper
  • soy sauce and toasted sesame seeds(optional)

    • Instructions

  • 1. Place rice and water in steamer and set to cook.
  • 2. While rice is cooking, whisk together vinegar, sugar and salt.
  • 3. Once rice is done, stir in vinegar mixture.
  • 4. You may serve this rice warm or cold, then top with veggies (you can also add soy sauce and tpasted sesame seeds on top!)
