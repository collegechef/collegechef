Sticky Rice

Time: 30 minutes

Cost: $4.17

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of uncooked rice
  • Canned or frozen vegetables
  • Soy sauce, to taste"

    • Instructions

    1. Steam your rice.

    2. Microwave your vegetables.

    3. Mix the vegetables, rice, and soy sauce together.
